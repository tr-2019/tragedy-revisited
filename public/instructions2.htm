Instructions for the game - Part 2

Please read them carefully


About money
All your fish will be sold immediately after you catch them (super fresh fish!). You receive 1 coin for every fish you caught. Your coins will be transformed into real € at the end of the game (1 coin equals 0.22€). And now, about the second part ...
In the second part of each round, the fisherwomen and -men are able to show their solidarity and donate some of their earnings to another player (2.5 coins). If you do not like to do so, you are free to keep all your hard-earned coins.

The mayor of the village encourages this friendly behavior by adding some money from the village's budget to each donation (The mayor adds another 1.5 coins to the 2.5 coins, which yields a donation of 4 ECU).

In each round, you and one of your colleagues are matched randomly. One colleague will be able to donate to you (or not), you will have the same choice and will be able to donate or not to him or her. Your colleague's decision of the last fishing round will be displayed to you and you can decide if he or she deserves your donation ... If you donate, your earnings will be reduced by 2.5 coins.

Please be aware: Your most recent fishing decision will also be displayed to your respective colleague (but to him or her only).

If you understood the instructions correctly press the "DONE" button to proceed to the game.
